Overview

This course is for you who want to expand your spiritual practice and abilities, but don’t have the time (or patience) to sift through a thousand books, teachers, and noise.

Together, we’ll read a small, curated set of texts — from ancient (including Egyptian) to modern — which are both highly inspirational and effective in practice.

We’ll be a group of fun and adventurous people who support each other. With the amount of help you’ll be getting, this will feel effortless.

You will learn to connect to the unseen realities through both day-to-day living and more structured practice. The magical approach applies to health, creativity, relationships; living in a more high energy – less limited – way.

Content & details

Some of the traditions & themes we'll touch upon are alchemy, metaphysics, renaissance magic, chaos magic, hermeticism, and esoteric Christianity.

We'll be reading from Marie-Louise von Franz, Austin Osman Spare, Carolyne Myss, Israel Regardie, Jane Roberts, as well as some unknown authors such as the Hermetica.

A few of the practices we'll do is dream work sigils, belief re-structuring and magical ceremonies.
