Overview

This course is for anyone who wants to expand their spiritual practice, but don’t have the time (or patience) to sift through a thousand books, teachers, and noise.

Together, we’ll read a small, curated set of texts — from ancient Egyptian scrolls to modern material — which are highly inspirational and works in practice.

We’ll be a group of fun and adventurous people who support each other in understanding and practicing. With the amount of help you’ll be getting, this will feel effortless.

You will learn to connect to the unseen realities through both day-to-day living and more structured practice. The effects translates into health, work, relationships, and quality of life.

Format

Early March to end of May 2026.

  • A balance of curated theory and practical applications.
  • Weekly check-ins with your fellow practitioners.
  • A private group chat for reflections and day to day help.
  • Shared notes to gather our learnings into a Book of Keys.
  • Access to 1-on-1 sessions for extra support.

If you are interested in in-person retreats and 1-on-1s, that will be offered later in 2026.
