Overview

Between January 5th 2026 and April 30th 2026 we will go through the Eight Gates of esotericism together. We will study different traditions of the hidden teachings and principles behind how reality takes shape.

  1. Alchemy
  2. Metaphysics
  3. Hermeticism
  4. Rituals & ceremonies
  5. The subtle body
  6. Inner Christianity
  7. Taoism
  8. The hero's journey

We will translate occult knowledge into practical terms of creativity, health, relationships, and our Great Work.

Format

This course is for you, who have an intuition that there is more to the universe than what we are told. This course gives you a structure, and a map, of the landscape of spiritual technologies.

  • A balance of curated theory and practical applications.
  • Weekly check-ins with your fellow practitioners.
  • A private, encrypted chat for reflections in the group.
  • Shared notes to gather our learnings into a Book of Keys.
  • Access to 1-on-1 sessions for extra support.

If you are interested in in-person retreats and 1-on-1s, that will be available later in 2026.
